ABOUT US

Student Developers Society

We are a community with a vision to bridge the gap between students and professionals. Our mission is to make students industry ready by helping them achieve professionalism. We actively conduct workshops and interactive sessions to help aid us in fulfilling our mission. We were one of the organizing communities of the first ever Rails Girls Kochi workshop along with Kerala Ruby Community. We are turning a year old and so far, the journey has been sweet.

THINGS WE CAN DO

Networking

Participate in meetups and workshops conducted in association with different communities to get to know interesting people while picking up new skills along the way.

Open Source Dev.

We are advocates of open source. Join us in events like Open Source Kerala to learn some interesting stuff about open source and be a part of other cool communities as well.

Meet Ups

Get involved in meetups of a wonderful community of developers and aspiring developers to share ideas and to know about the best practices in the development world.

Skill Enhancement

Join in on our workshops, talks, interactive sessions and a lot more to pick up a new skill or to enhance your skill through our well educated instructors.
